Multi stakeholder meeting in Johnstown Castle to talk about Water Quality and Sustainable Intensification with Pilot Scale bioreactor visit
27/3/2017 - 27/3/2017

Stakeholders:Department of Agriculture, Department of Environmnet, EPA, County Council, Irish Water

This face to face discussion group was used to introduce the Inspiration ITN project in the context of water quality research being conducted in Ireland. As part of the morning there was a visit to the pilot scale denitrification bioreactor used to clean drainage water. A Q & A session was conducted in the field.
